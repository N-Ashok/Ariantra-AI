Who We Are

Ariantra is a product of Ariantra AI Foundry Private Limited, a company incorporated in India. We run a game-building bootcamp for kids aged 8–14, where children build their own games using AI — guided by trained mentors.

When these terms say "Ariantra", "we", "us", or "our", we mean Ariantra AI Foundry Private Limited.

02

The Service

Ariantra provides:

  • One-on-one online mentored sessions where kids build games using AI
  • A platform to save, view, and share games built during sessions
  • Session notes and progress summaries shared with parents after each session

Sessions are conducted online via video call. The first session is free with no commitment required.

Children cannot enter into contracts. All agreements under these terms are between Ariantra and the parent or legal guardian.

04

Sessions, Booking & Payment

Sessions are booked via WhatsApp or the Ariantra platform. Each session is:

  • 45–60 minutes, conducted fully online, one-on-one
  • Scheduled at a mutually agreed time between the mentor and parent
  • Paid in advance unless otherwise agreed in writing

Payment is accepted via Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking). Session fees are communicated at the time of booking and may be revised with 30 days notice to existing students.

The first session is free of charge. No payment information is required to book the free session.

Cancellation & Refunds

If Ariantra cancels a session: A full credit or cash refund will be provided within 14 days. No penalty to the parent.

If a parent cancels a session: Credit or rescheduling is offered at Ariantra's discretion, depending on notice given. We aim to be fair and handle each situation individually.

If a parent discontinues with unused prepaid sessions: A refund of the unused amount will be processed within 30 days of a written request, less an administrative fee of 10%.

For any refund or cancellation queries, contact us via WhatsApp or email. Written requests can be sent via WhatsApp message or email to hello@ariantra.com.
