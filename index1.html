How it works
What happens in a session
1

They decide what to build

First question every session: "What do you want to build today?" No syllabus. No assigned projects. If they can imagine it, we build it.

2

They build it — with AI as their developer

The kid describes what they want. The AI builds it. The kid plays it, finds what's missing, and keeps going. No typing code. Pure creative thinking.

3

They explain it to you

Last 15 minutes: you join. They walk you through what they built and why. This is non-negotiable — explaining what you made is how you understand what you learned.

4

They tell us what's next

One question at the end: "What do you want to add next time?" That answer is what brings them back.

Our one rule as teachers:
We never touch the keyboard.

We don't give answers. We ask questions. "What do you think is missing?" "What should happen when the player does that?" The kid figures it out — we just help them think.
