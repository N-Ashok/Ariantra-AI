Who We Are

Ariantra AI Foundry Private Limited is the data controller responsible for your personal data. We run a game-building bootcamp for children aged 8–14, operating online across India.

When this policy says "Ariantra", "we", "us", or "our", it means Ariantra AI Foundry Private Limited.

This policy applies to all personal data collected through our website, WhatsApp communication, and the Ariantra platform. It should be read alongside our Terms & Conditions.

03

Children's Data

Important — Please Read

Ariantra's service is designed for children aged 8–14. Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act), Section 9, processing personal data of a child requires verifiable parental consent.

By enrolling your child and agreeing to these terms, you — as the parent or legal guardian — provide this consent on behalf of your child. We do not collect data from children directly. All data is collected from and through the parent.

We do not serve behavioural advertising to children. We do not track children's activity outside of our platform. We do not build profiles on children for any purpose other than delivering the Ariantra service.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly. To report such a concern, contact our Grievance Officer.

06

Your Rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 and the IT (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules 2011, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a summary of what personal data we hold about you and your child.

Right to Correct

Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erase

Request deletion of your personal data. We will action this within 30 days. Note: games built during sessions are Ariantra's IP and are not subject to erasure requests.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for processing your child's data at any time. This will end the service relationship.

Right to Grievance Redressal

Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer. We will respond within 48 hours and resolve within 30 days.

Right to Nominate

Nominate another individual to exercise your rights on your behalf in the event of death or incapacity.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Grievance Officer via WhatsApp or email. We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours.

08

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. These include:

  • HTTPS encryption across all Ariantra web properties
  • Access to personal data limited to Ariantra mentors and administrators on a need-to-know basis
  • Payment data handled entirely by Razorpay — Ariantra does not store card or bank details
  • Session data stored on secured servers with access controls

No system is completely secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, contact our Grievance Officer immediately.

09

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses the following cookies and tracking tools:

  • Mixpanel — measures anonymised usage across all ariantra.com pages and published game pages (pages visited, clicks, game plays and how long games are played). Typed text is never captured, no names or emails are sent, and raw IP addresses are not stored. On site pages (never on game pages) it also records sessions with ALL text masked and media/embedded games blocked.

We do not use advertising cookies or track users across third-party websites.

Our website does not currently display a cookie consent banner. We will add one as Indian cookie consent regulations are clarified under the DPDP Act framework.
